The children in Iraq face a crisis that has been unheard of for decades — the potiental extinction of an entire people group. Yazidi and Christian children in the Middle East face unspeakable horrors that no child should ever have to experience. Young girls are priced and sold as slaves to older men. Young boys are turned into suicide bombers.

These children should be going to school. Learning to read. Playing outside. Dreaming about their futures. Not being used as pawns in a religious war. What would you do if these were your children?

The holocaust of our generation is going on right now. We have to stand up and fight against it. Find your representatives below and get in touch with them now.
